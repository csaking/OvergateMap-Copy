User Guide

The map can be navigated using your mouse to point and click on markers within the stores, drag around the map, or to change the floor of Overgate you are currently viewing through a slider on the right of the map. The mouse wheel will allow you to zoom in and out of the map.

Popup

1. Stats tab

Clicking on a marker will display its pop-up. The pop-up contains information related to that store includes an image of the store, brief description, address, opening Times. The current visitors, population density, density rating of that store and the total visitors of the day also been included. The pop-up contains this information inside stats tabs, which can be navigated by clicking their heading with the mouse.

2. Visualisation tab

On the second tab of a pop-up you will be able to see a graph that updates as it receives information. This graph shows you how many people are in that store at that time. The system will use this information when calculating people density, which you will be able to see by the highlighted colour of the store marker changing. Red means high people density which is busy for that store, yellow means average, and green means quiet.

Overlay

In addition, the Overlay at the top-right corner of the map that would display the information of the whole Overgate. The information includes number of current visitors, shops and floors. There is also a pie chart which made up of three colours (red, yellow, green) shows the people density rate of the Overgate. The percentage of each colour represent the percentage of stores are in the certain people density rate.
